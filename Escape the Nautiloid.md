# Prologue: Escape the Nautiloid

## Map

https://mapgenie.io/baldurs-gate-3/maps/nautiloid

## Requirements

No requirements.

## Reference

[IGN Guide](https://www.ign.com/wikis/baldurs-gate-3/Prologue:_Escape_the_Nautiloid)

## Steps

1. Check Nursery Pod in the middle of the room (Investigation)

2. Check Restoration Chamber towards room's north end (Restores Health)

3. Head through the door marked Sphincter and **keep an eye out for lootable chests**

4. Hear begging voice. The location of this voice is on the platform to the west of where you've walked in. Approach de-scalped person to start a cutscene.

5. **Recruit "Us" to simplify combat.**

6. Meet Lae'zel

7. **Re-enter the ship to start battle with Imps.** Dip arrows in environmental fire to increase damage. Middle of the room has highground.

8. Use Restoration Chamber nearby.

9. Climb Arterial Flesh. Cultists are not hostile unless you hit the middle button in the control panel. **Right-hand button will execute the Cultists.**

10. **Rescue Shadowheart**: find Eldritch Rune in the pockets of a dead Thrall. Loot the room (Gold Key, opens Elaborate Reliquary). Interact with the control panel and the Rune. Speak with Shadowheart.

11. Head south, use Restoration Chamber.

12. Turn West, go towards the Helm

13. **Kill or Disarm Zhalk (Command + Drop Weapon)**. Or skip him if you are a pussy.

14. Activate the transponder
