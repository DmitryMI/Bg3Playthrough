## Steps

1. Get close to Mirkon to start a fight with Harpies.

2. **Use immobilizing spells** on Harpies, they like to fly close.

3. **Don't let Mirkon to come close to singing Harpy**, it will kill the boy. Break this Harpy's concentration to prevent this.

4. Calm down Mirkon.

5. Speak to Doni (near Dragon's Lair).

6. Once inside the hideout, speak to Mol.

7. Agree to steal the sacred idol, **a very risky quest**.
