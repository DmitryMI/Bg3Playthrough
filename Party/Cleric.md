# Cleric - Light Domain

## Reference

[Light Domain Cleric Build](https://gamestegy.com/post/bg3/872/light-domain-cleric-build)

## Race

Pick any. The best picks are:

- Wood (Half-)Elf for increased movement speed (good for Spirit Guardians).
- High (Half-)Elf for race cantrip
- Asmodeus Tiefling - additional fire spells.

## Level 1

Subclass: [Light Domain](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Light_Domain)

Deity: pick any.

Background: Acolyte.

### Abilities

- 8 STR - not relevant
- 15+1 DEX - for AC
- 15 CON - helps to maintain concentrations
- 8 INT - not relevant
- 15+2 WIS - main spellcasting ability
- 8 CHA - not relevant

### Skills

- Medicine (no one else in the party has it)

### Cantrips

Priority list:

- [Sacred Flame](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sacred_Flame) - ranged damage spell. Scales with level.
- [Produce Flame](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Produce_Flame) - deals low damage when thrown. Uses Attack Roll. Scales with level.
- [Guidance](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Guidance) - target gets +1d4 bonus to Ability Checks.

### Spells (non-selectable):

- [Warding Flare](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Warding_Flare) - self-defence spell that applies DIS on enemy attacks on you.
- [Light](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Light) - make objects shed light. Useful in dark areas.
- [Burning Hands](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Burning_Hands) - short-range AoE damage.
- [Faerie Fire​](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Faerie_Fire) - gives ADV on attack rolls on enemies in AoE.

### Prepared Spells

Pick from priority list:

- [Guiding Bolt](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Guiding_Bolt) - ranged single-target spell that makes next attack roll on the target have ADV. **Must have.**
- [Bless](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Bless) - up to 3 allies gain +1d4 bonus to attack rolls and saving throws.
- [Command](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Command) - very useful control spell.
- [Healing Word​](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Healing_Word) - best mass-healing spell in the game.
- [Shield of Faith](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Shield_of_Faith) - ally's AC +2.
- [Create or Destroy Water](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Create_or_Destroy_Water) - must have spell to buff allied lightning and cold spells.
- [Sanctuary](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sanctuary) - make ally invulnerable as bonus action.
- [Inflict Wounds](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Inflict_Wounds) - high damage, but melee range. *Only pick if the party has insufficient firepower.*

## Level 2

### Spells (non-selectable)

> These spells are cast using [Channel Divinity Charge](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Channel_Divinity_Charge) (replenished with Short and Long Rests).

- [Turn Undead](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Turn_Undead) - mass-control spell against undead.
- [Radiance of the Dawn](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Radiance_of_the_Dawn) - removes Darkness and deals decent damage to all enemies around the caster.

## Level 3

### Spells (non-selectable)

- [Flaming Sphere](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Flaming_Sphere) - summons a controllable sphere that deals damage around itself.
- [Scorching Ray](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Scorching_Ray) - multi-target ranged damage spell.

### Spells (selectable)

Pick from priority list:

- [Warding Bond​](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Warding_Bond) - ally gains resistance to all damage and +1 bonus to AC and Saving Throws. When ally is damages, you receive the same amount.
- [Spiritual Weapon](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Spiritual_Weapon) - controllable unit that deals damage. Usefull against some bosses.
- [Protection from Poison](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Protection_from_Poison) - specifically useful against spiders.
- [Lesser Restoration](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Lesser_Restoration) - cures a couple of status effects in a melee range.
- [Hold Person](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Hold_Person) - bard will get it on Level 4.
- [Enhance Ability](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Enhance_Ability) - bard will get it on Level 3.

## Level 4

Feat: Ability Improvement. +1 CON, +1 WIS.

Cantrip: Reference guide suggests [Thaumaturgy](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Thaumaturgy), but I think [Resistance (Cantrip)](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Resistance_(Cantrip)) may be better, despite requiring concentration.

## Level 5

Feature: [Destroy Undead](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Destroy_Undead) - buffed version of Turn Undead that also deals damage to them.

### Spells (non-selectable)

- [Daylight](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Daylight) - global illumination, dispells Darkness, weakens creatures with [Sunlight Sensitivity](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sunlight_Sensitivity) (many Drow, Drider, Duergar, Kobold, Shadow, and Vampire creatures).
- [Fireball](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Fireball) - huge AoE damage.

### Spells (selectable)

Pick from priority list:

- [Mass Healing Word](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Mass_Healing_Word) - **must have.**
- [Spirit Guardians](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Spirit_Guardians) - strong self-defence and AoE spell, but requires you to be on the frontline.
- [Bestow Curse](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Bestow_Curse) - single-target disable spell. I am not sure how strong it is.
- [Animate Dead](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Animate_Dead)
- [Revivify](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Revivify) - revives ally.
- [Remove Curse](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Remove_Curse) - removes curses
- [Glyph of Warding](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Glyph_of_Warding) - AoE damage or disable. Bard will get it on Level 5.

## Level 6

Respec: [Sorcerer (Storm Sorcery)](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sorcerer) Level 1, then Cleric Level 5. *Do in correct order!*

The main (and the only actually) reason to dip into Sorcerer is to get [Tempestuous Magic](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Tempestuous_Magic) that allows you to fly as a bonus action after casting any Level 1 spell. This will allow you to apply [Spirit Guardians](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Spirit_Guardians) to as many enemies as possible.

### Cantrips (Sorcerer)

Pick any, they all are either very weak at this point, or allies already have them.

### Spells (Sorcerer)

- [Shield](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Shield_(spell)) - self-defence spell.
- [Magic Missile](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Magic_Missile) - small, but guaranteed damage.

## Level 7

Feature: [Improved Warding Flare](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Improved_Warding_Flare) - defencive feature that costs a reaction.

## Level 8

All not very strong, pick any or none:

Feat: Either Ability Improvement (+2 WIS), or [Alert](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Alert)

- [Planar Ally](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Planar_Ally) - pretty good summon

## Tactics

For healing use only (Mass) Healing Word. They are cheap and cost only bonus action. Also they apply buffs provided by your equipment to all allies at once.

Use your mobility to apply Spirit Guardians to as many enemies as possible.

Stack Arcane Acuity by using Scorching Ray and Hat of Fire Acuity to boost your Spell Save DC. This will allow most of your spells have near-100% success rate.

Use Bless before combat.
