## Requirements

No requirements.

## Rewards/Loot

1. The Watcher's Guide (Spear, Uncommon)

2. The Amulet of Lost Voices (Rare)

## Steps

1. **Speak to the bandits exploring the area outside of the ruins to get the quest.**

2. **Kill them all!**

3. Enter the Crypt. Watch for traps.

4. **Loot the skeletons!**

5. **Activate a button** to the left of large statue to reanimate the skeletons and start the battle.

6. Head the newely-opened chamber to **find Withers**

7. Loot his sarcophagus and the heavy chest for **Speak with the Dead amulet**.

8. After quest completed, Withers will join the Camp.
