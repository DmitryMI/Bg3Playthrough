##  Reference

## Race

> If played as Astarion, race and starting features are unchangeable.

### [High Elf](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Elf)

- Proficiency with Perception.

- Proficiency with Longswords, Shortswords, Longbows, and Shortbows.

- Darkvision (12m).

- ADV for Charmed Saving Throws.

- Cannot be put to sleep.

### Starting Cantrip
+1 Cantrip from Wizard spell list (use INT). 

Select 1 from priority list:

- [Minor Illusion](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Minor_Illusion)
- [Friends](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Friends) - **difficult to use on Tactician and higher.**
- [Fire Bolt](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Fire_Bolt)

## Level 1

Class - [Bard](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Bard).

Feature: [Bardic Inspiration](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Bardic_Inspiration_(class_action)) - +1d6 bonus to next Attack Roll, Ability Check or Saving Throw.

### Cantrips

Pick 2 from priority list:

- [Vicious Mockery](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Vicious_Mockery)
- [Friends](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Friends)

### Spells

Pick 4 from priority list:

- [Sleep](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sleep)
- [Dissonant Whispers​](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Dissonant_Whispers)
- [Healing Word​](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Healing_Word) - if nobody else has it
- [Heroism](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Heroism) - makes target immune to Frightened and gives 5 temp. hit points.
- [Longstrider](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Longstrider) - if nobody else has it
- [Faerie Fire](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Faerie_Fire) - *Cleric will unavoidably have it on Level 1.*

### Starting instrument

- Pick any

## Level 5

### Spells

- [Glyph of Warding](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Glyph_of_Warding) - can be used as [Glyph of Warding: Sleep](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Glyph_of_Warding:_Sleep), mass-disable spell.
- Replace Faerie Fire, Longstrider or Heroism with [Plant Growth](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Plant_Growth) - mass slowdown.

## Level 6

Feature: [Countercharm](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Countercharm)

### Spells

Pick as many as allowed from priority list:

- [Hypnotic Pattern](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Hypnotic_Pattern) - mass disable
- [Hunger of Hadar](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Hunger_of_Hadar) - mass damage and blindness. Combine with Ranger's [Plant Growth](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Plant_Growth).
- [Counterspell](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Counterspell) - stop enemy's spell with a reaction.
- [Sleet Storm](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Sleet_Storm) - mass slowdown. *Synergizes with Hunger of Hadar, but cannot be cast together with it due to concentration.*

## Tactics

### Counterspell + Cutting Words

Cancels enemy spells. Not recommended to negate offencive spells, **negate control spells like Silence or Confusion instead**.

**Duellist's Prerogative** gives another reaction point to use per turn.

### Hunger of Hadar

Use Eldritch Blast with Repelling Blast to push enemies into Hunger of Hadar.

### Enemy Counterspell

Use party members to cast useless spells to bait enemy casters to waster Counterspell.
