## Rewards

None.

## Steps

1. Inside Druid's Grove go to Makeshift Prison

2. Interrogate the goblin to learn of a potential healer that may help to remove your tadpole.

3. Accept her quest.

4. Pick cage lock, smash it or use [Iron Cell Key](https://bg3.wiki/wiki/Iron_Cell_Key) (**don't get caught!**) - 15 XP

5. Stealthly escape the Grove by jumping the rocks behind the cage. Follow Sazza, kill goblins in the cave

6. Decide whether or not to save the unconscious man.

7. Climb up the cliffs or head up the steps to the higher section, then head south to another stone door

8. Speak with Sazza

9. Meet with Sazza later in Goblin Camp. She will help you to meet with Goblin Leaders.

10. When Minthara tries to kill Sazza, **save her**.
