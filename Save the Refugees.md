## Rewards

1. Komira’s Locket (Dancing Lights)

## Steps

1. Approach the Druid's Grove to trigger a **fight**.

2. Speak with Zevlor

3. Go to druids Grove

4. Along the path, [meet Arabella](https://www.ign.com/wikis/baldurs-gate-3/Save_Arabella).

5. Speak to her parents (Komira and Locke), **offer your help**.

6. The door to Kagha’s chamber will be on the northwest side of this clearing, but be sure to **wake up the bear** sleeping on the lift on the southwest side.

7. Enter Kagha's room, **Save Arabella**

8. Go into the northwest room of her chambers and read the Rite of Thorns on the desk in the middle of the room to start [Investigate Kagha](https://www.ign.com/wikis/baldurs-gate-3/Investigate_Kagha) quest.

9. Find a chest in the Western part of Emerald Grove

10. Pickpocket Kagha for a key or pick the lock on this chest

11. Read the letter inside the chest. This will pinpoint location where Kagha can be killed later **without starting civil war**.

*Siding will Goblins will preven Wyll, Karlach and Halsin from joining the party. Next steps assume you want to be The Good Guy.*
